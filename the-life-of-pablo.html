THE LIFE OF

PABLO

In this album, Kanye West revisits his roots by reintroducing traditional hip-hop beats and samples, along with a return to his iconic rapping style. This throwback to his earlier sound is accompanied by raw, introspective lyrics that confront the darker aspects of fame, betrayal, and the challenge of finding genuine friends. One particularly striking moment comes when Kanye candidly shares a personal story about his cousin, who stole his MacBook containing intimate content, forcing him to pay $250,000 to retrieve it. Kanye also reflects on his failures as a family member, admitting that he often prioritizes work and partying over spending time with his loved ones. He bluntly describes himself as a "deadbeat cousin," acknowledging the sacrifices and regrets that come with his relentless pursuit of success. This album, with its blend of classic Kanye sound and unfiltered honesty, offers a poignant exploration of the internal and external conflicts that define his life.
